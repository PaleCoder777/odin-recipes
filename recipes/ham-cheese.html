Ham and Cheese Sandwich

Why this recipe?

Another sandwich recipe for the books.

Very simple, very quick, very cheap

Ingredients

  • Sliced Bread
  • Sliced ham
  • American Cheese slices

How to start eating in minutes!

  1. Get set up with a plate and pat it with 2 pairs of sliced bread.
  2. Get a slice of ham and cheese and pile it onto 2 slices.
  3. Close them with other slice to make the sandwich, and that is it!
  4. Enjoy!
