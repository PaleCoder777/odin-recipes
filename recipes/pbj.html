Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Why this recipe?

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a simple staple in many households. It mixes the grainy texture of peanut butter with the sweetness of jelly to create a combo that can't be beat.

It uses so little ingredients, while being quick to make, and can even be eaten on the go.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

How to slap together the PB & J!

  1. Set up your area with a plate, 2 knives, 2 pairs of bread, peanut butter, and a jelly of your choice.
  2. With one knife, spread as much peanut butter as you want on 2 bread slices.
  3. With the other knife, do the same but this time with some of that sweet jelly.
  4. Slap a peanut butter and jelly bread slice together and that is it!
  5. Enjoy!
